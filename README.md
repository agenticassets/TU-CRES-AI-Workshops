# TU Center for Real Estate Studies (CRES) Professional Development Seminars

**Official Website:** [https://real-estate.utulsa.edu/tu-cres-professional-development-seminars/](https://real-estate.utulsa.edu/tu-cres-professional-development-seminars/)

## Spring 2026 | University of Tulsa

The TU Center for Real Estate Studies is proud to offer a specialized series of professional development seminars for the broader Tulsa real estate community (Residential & Commercial). Led by **Dr. Cayman Seagraves** and **Dr. Meagan McCollum**, these sessions are designed to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and practical application for agents, brokers, and property managers across the industry.

---

## Seminar Schedule & Series Overview

All seminars are held from **6:00 PM – 8:15 PM** in **Helmerich Hall**, University of Tulsa.

### 1. Introduction to Using AI in Real Estate
*   **Date:** Wednesday, February 4, 2026
*   **Registration Deadline:** Feb. 1st
*   **Focus:** Demystifying AI for agents, brokers, and property managers. Practical tools for market research, due diligence, and client communication.
*   **Reference:** `workshop-1-info.md`, `Introduction-to-using-AI-in-Real-Estate.md`

### 2. Becoming a Data-Driven Real Estate Decision Maker with AI
*   **Date:** Wednesday, March 4, 2026
*   **Registration Deadline:** March 2nd
*   **Focus:** Moving beyond the basics into data analytics. Portfolio optimization, underwriting, and risk assessment across residential and commercial sectors.
*   **Reference:** `workshop-2-info.md`

### 3. Driving ROI in Real Estate with AI Workflows
*   **Date:** Wednesday, April 1, 2026
*   **Registration Deadline:** March 30th
*   **Focus:** Building repeatable, scalable AI-powered workflows that directly enhance profitability and reduce operational friction.
*   **Reference:** `workshop-3-info.md`
