## Workshop Overview

In our final professional development offering for Spring 2026, participants learn how to turn artificial intelligence into a measurable return on investment. Driving ROI in Real Estate with AI Workflows focuses on building repeatable, efficient, and scalable processes that directly enhance profitability, reduce friction, and create long-term competitive advantage across real estate operations.

This advanced session highlights how AI-powered workflows can streamline deal sourcing, accelerate underwriting, optimize property management, and elevate marketing performance. Attendees will learn how to map their own business processes, identify high-impact opportunities for AI intervention, and design workflows that produce consistent, data-backed outcomes. We also cover ROI tracking methods, implementation best practices, and strategies for integrating AI tools into team workflows and client-facing deliverables.
