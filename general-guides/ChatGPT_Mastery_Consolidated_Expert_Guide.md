## Advanced Prompt Engineering Techniques

### Multi-Layer Prompt Architecture

**RACE Framework** **[Gemini Lines 180-184]**:
- **Role**: Define AI's persona and expertise level
- **Action**: Specify the task or objective
- **Context**: Provide background information and constraints
- **Expectation**: Detail output format and success criteria

**CRISPE Framework** **[Gemini Lines 185-190]**:
- **Capacity/Role**: AI's role and capabilities
- **Insight**: Key information or problem statement
- **Statement**: Specific request or task
- **Personality**: Desired tone and communication style
- **Experiment**: Alternative approaches or variations to explore

**Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Techniques** **[Gemini Lines 196-201; Perplexity Lines 126-131]**:
- **Zero-shot CoT**: "Let's think step by step" for immediate reasoning activation
- **Few-shot CoT**: 2-3 examples demonstrating desired reasoning process
- **Self-Criticism**: Instructions for AI to review and improve its own responses

## Memory & Context Management

### Dual-Level Memory System

**Session Memory** **[Perplexity Lines 152-167]**:
- 8,000 tokens per conversation (approximately 6,000-6,500 words)
- Structure conversations within token limits
- Use summarization for context compression when approaching limits

**Permanent Memory**:
- Unlimited capacity for persistent memories
- Strategic additions for professional details, preferences, methodologies
- Regular audits and relevance updates
- Organization by project, client, or functional area

### Memory Commands & Optimization

**Available Commands** **[OpenAI Lines 61-64; Perplexity Line 166]**:
- "Remember that my organization uses AP style."
- "Forget my phone number."
- "List what you remember about me."
- "SAVE TO MEMORY: [topic]" and "RECALL: [topic]"

### Privacy & Data Management

**Security Protocols** **[Gemini Lines 160-162; Perplexity Lines 169-180]**:

- Temporary Chat for sensitive discussions (no history retention)
